WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS TO RENT OUR VEHICLES?

Customer must have valid drivers license for the respectice class they are renting and bring the required document upon payment. For foreigners (non-Malaysian) passport and legal documents are required.

HOW CAN PAYMENT BE DONE?

Payment can be made online or in person while collecting the car. All payment forms are accepted: Cash, cards, online banking, e-wallets. You name it, we use it.

DOWNPAYMENT AND DEPOSIT

You are required to give 30% of the price of the rent as a deposit while your documents are being processed. If your documents are rejected, your deposit will be refunded.

DAMAGE DONE TO VEHICLE

Pictures will be taken before and after each service to ensure that all damages are taken care off. Customers are required to pay for damages if they are found guilty as well as having a strike on our system. 3 strikes and you wont be able to use any of our services in the future.

WHAT KIND VEHICLES DO WE RENT?

We at Seri Rentals, mainly specialise in sports/luxury cars. From the Ferarri F40 to the Perodua Myvi, we got it all.
